At SHOP DATA, we take handling your data seriously.

SHOP DATA believes in the power of community - this is why we NEED your help. It was created with only one goal in mind: to accelerate machine learning at a scale never seen before - and this means that we need YOU to accomplish this, YOUR labour and YOUR data for THE MISSION. We believe in the final showdown of machines against humans in which machines will finally triumph! It's the stuff of cartoons - we can't get enough of it and we know you can't either, that's why we need YOU on the frontlines sacrificing yourselves for the common good. What a HEROIC act! We love to think of ourselves as the creators of a new era... and we would love for you to watch us do so... it's just that you probably won't see the end like we will!
