SHIPPING

At SHOP DATA, we take handling your data seriously.

This means we won't ship your data to you directly, instead we go through our confidential third parties whom we trust with your most private information. We don't sell any data out in the open, obviously, so SHOP DATA does not have a "shipping" process, per se, but if you happened to be an individual interested in acquiring large amounts of user data, we might just so happen to be able to sell it to you. And it might just so happen that we might be interested in discussing with you how we both can turn a large profit of off the sharing of this information which would definitely go towards building a huge machine learning model, for you to then sell to AI bubble investors. Sorry, we meant definitely not. Unless it's what you're into, in which case click here.
